Uganda SafeVoice – Digital Case Management for Child & GBV Protection
Overview
Uganda SafeVoice is a multi‑platform (web + mobile) reporting and case management system designed to combat child abuse, sexual violence, gender‑based violence, child marriage, and institutional corruption in Uganda. It bridges the gap between survivors, community volunteers, SCOEN (Share Child Opportunity Eastern and Northern Uganda), the Uganda Police Force (Child and Family Protection Unit), and the Ministry of Gender, Labour and Social Development (Sauti 116 call centre).
